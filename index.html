Community Heroes (CH/CHs) are well-selected MLBB players whose main task
is to create, maintain, and grow a community of MLBB players in certain cities
or provinces. The CHs organizes official Moonton events such as small scale on
-ground gatherings with friendly MLBB tournaments and some various online
MLBB tournaments. The CHs also serve as Moonton's reliable manpower to
handle various official MLBB events.

While the Community Heroes generally are friendly and reliable people with
integrity, they also do live simple lives like everyone else. They aren't perfect but
they do their best
